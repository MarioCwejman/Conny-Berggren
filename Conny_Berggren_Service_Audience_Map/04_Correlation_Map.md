# Correlation Map

| Service | Core audience | Secondary audience / referrer | Why they buy it | Credibility angle to emphasize | Optimal contact medium | Why that medium fits |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Entreprenadbesiktning` | Byggherre, fastighetsägare, projektutvecklare, BRF, kommun, privat byggherre | Entreprenör, projektledare | To verify delivery, document defects, or close out a project cleanly. | Independent assessment, contract understanding, and structured defect documentation. | `Phone first, then email documents` | Timing is often tight. You usually need a quick read on project status, then contract docs, drawings, and dates by email. |
| `Kontrollansvarig (KA)` | Byggherre, privat byggherre, BRF, fastighetsägare, projektutvecklare | Arkitekt, projektledare | To satisfy PBL process and keep the project moving toward startbesked and slutbesked. | Certification, process reliability, and calm guidance through permit and close-out steps. | `Email first, then booked call` | The role is document-heavy. Best first step is to receive permit status, drawings, municipality, and timeline, then discuss scope in a short call. |
| `Bas-P` | Byggherre, projektutvecklare, kommun, större fastighetsägare | Projektledare, projekteringsgrupp | To set up risk-conscious planning early and avoid avoidable work-environment issues later. | Risk-aware planning, coordination discipline, and authority early in the project. | `Booked call or meeting first` | This is an early-stage coordination role. A meeting is better than a generic form because the client needs to explain project type, risk level, and team setup. |
| `Bas-U` | Byggherre, projektutvecklare, kommun, större fastighetsägare | Huvudentreprenör, platschef, projektledare | To coordinate work-environment issues during production and keep the site functioning safely. | Operational site realism, decisive communication, and safety coordination. | `Phone first, then meeting or project email thread` | The work is operational and time-sensitive. It usually needs fast alignment with production planning and site actors. |
| `Projektledning / byggprojektledning` | Projektutvecklare, fastighetsägare, kommun, BRF med större renovering | Konsult, arkitekt | To drive a project that the client cannot or does not want to manage alone. | Senior oversight, structure, and stakeholder management. | `Booked intro meeting` | Scope can vary a lot. A meeting is the fastest way to understand mandate, budget, procurement stage, and team gaps. |
| `Sakkunnig- och tviststöd / expertutlåtanden` | Advokat, byggherre, fastighetsägare, projektutvecklare, BRF, contractor in dispute | Ombud, försäkringsgivare, projektledare | To explain technical issues, industry practice, defects, causation, or reasonableness in a dispute. | Independence, written precision, and judgment that can withstand scrutiny. | `Direct phone first, then secure email of case material` | These matters are sensitive, urgent, and fact-heavy. A direct call helps qualify the case quickly before documents are shared. |
| `Senior extern specialist i komplexa projekt / problemprojekt` | Projektutvecklare, fastighetsägare, BRF, kommun | Projektledare, advokat, konsult | To stabilize a messy or high-stakes project without hiring a full new organization. | Calm senior diagnosis, fast stabilization, and credibility in messy situations. | `Direct phone plus fast follow-up meeting or site visit` | These assignments are rarely cleanly scoped at first contact. Speed, trust, and context-gathering matter more than a standard form. |

## Audience-to-Service Fit

| Audience | Best-fit services |
| --- | --- |
| `Privat byggherre / småhuskund` | Entreprenadbesiktning, KA |
| `BRF` | Entreprenadbesiktning, KA, projektledning, problemprojektstöd |
| `Fastighetsägare / förvaltare` | Entreprenadbesiktning, KA, Bas-P, Bas-U, projektledning, tviststöd |
| `Projektutvecklare / kommersiell beställare` | Entreprenadbesiktning, Bas-P, Bas-U, projektledning, tviststöd, problemprojektstöd |
| `Kommun / offentlig beställare` | Entreprenadbesiktning, KA, Bas-P, Bas-U, projektledning |
| `Entreprenör / huvudentreprenör` | Bas-U coordination interface, joint inspection matters, dispute or expert support |
| `Advokat / ombud` | Sakkunnig- och tviststöd / expertutlåtanden |
| `Konsult / projekt- eller byggledare` | Referral source into any of the above, especially specialist inspections, BAS, and problemprojektstöd |

Note:

- Contact-medium guidance is a recommendation based on buying logic, urgency, and document sensitivity.
